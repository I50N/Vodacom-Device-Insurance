However, if your device is beyond repair (as determined by an authorized Vodacom repair center), Vodacom Insurance will replace your device with a new or similar device of the same value, subject to the Maximum Limit of Indemnity.

Please also note that Vodacom Insurance has the right to change your premium at any time, subject to 30 days written notice to you.
With an excess applicable of 10% of repair cost (Subject to a minimum of R100 and a maximum of R1000) and 15% of the replacement cost (Subject to a minimum of R100).
The excess payable for replacements is 15% of the replacement cost of the insured device. Your excess will be doubled if your claim occurs within 12 months of a previous successful claim, under the same benefit option on this Policy. Please note that your premiums are not based on the value of the phone and at the claims stage, the settlement amount is based on the value of the phone at that time.
